Usage Instructions

How to use this product

Directions

  • Apply liberally 15 minutes before sun exposure
  • Reapply at least every 2 hours
  • Reapply immediately after swimming, sweating, or towel-drying
  • Children under 6 months: Ask a doctor

Application Tips

  • Use approximately 1 ounce (a shot glass) for full body
  • For face: use a nickel-sized amount
  • Don't forget ears, back of neck, tops of feet
  • Can be worn under makeup
Warnings & Safety

Safety information

For external use only. Do not use on damaged or broken skin. Keep out of eyes. Stop use if rash occurs. Keep out of reach of children.

Questions About This Product

What consumers ask

Does Neutrogena Ultra Sheer contain oxybenzone?
Yes. The Ultra Sheer Dry-Touch SPF 55 includes oxybenzone (4%). For an oxybenzone-free option, consider Neutrogena Sheer Zinc (100% mineral zinc oxide).
Is this sunscreen suitable for sensitive skin?
It is dermatologist-tested and non-comedogenic. However, it contains chemical UV filters. For sensitive skin, try the Sheer Zinc line with 100% mineral active ingredients.
Can I use this as a daily facial sunscreen?
Yes. The Ultra Sheer Dry-Touch formula is designed for daily facial use. It is oil-free, non-comedogenic, and has a lightweight, matte finish that works well under makeup.
What is Helioplex technology?
Helioplex is Neutrogena's patented stabilization technology that maintains the effectiveness of UV-filtering ingredients during prolonged sun exposure.
